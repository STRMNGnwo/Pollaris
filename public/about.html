About Us

Project Hermes is a poll based social networking application that serves to connect people through their respective interests. A user will generate a poll, using a seamless UI and thus will release it either to their added friend list or to a greater radius of people. This radius of which be carefully measured will allow for other users nearby to participate in these polls.

The main motivation for this web application is to provide another medium for communication amongst people around the world, something different to the normalities of the currently popular social networks. It will serve to be as efficient as possible and coordinate numerous opinions and ideas in one enriched place, so that people can constantly be connecting through their ideas and opinions and not because of solely their appearance and name.
